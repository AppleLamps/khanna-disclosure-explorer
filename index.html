Ro Khanna — Financial Disclosure Explorer

Independent OCR transcription of Rep. Ro Khanna's (CA-17) 2024 annual Financial Disclosure Report. Source: official filing (Clerk of the House, PDF). All dollar figures are the statutory ranges reported on the form, not exact values.

Why this site exists: Rep. Khanna disclosed at least $98.7 million in holdings as 333 pages of hand-delivered paper — unsearchable scanned images — rather than filing through the House's electronic system. This site makes those pages readable, searchable, and analyzable.

        Methodology. The official filing is a 333-page paper scan with no machine-readable text. Each page is OCR-transcribed with a large vision model reading the full page plus four high-resolution crops, cross-checked against a conventional OCR engine (Tesseract). Readings that could not be resolved with confidence are flagged inline in the Document tab, and an independent second-pass verification of every page is part of the pipeline. All dollar figures are the statutory ranges the form requires, not exact values.
